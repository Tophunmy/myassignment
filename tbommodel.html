Frequently Asked Questions

How does TBOM work?
TBOM provides a platform for mentees to find mentors particularly those in a specific field or expertise.


How much does this service cost?
The platform is free of charge


Who are eligible as mentors and mentees?
Mentors are professionals with years of experience in a particular area of research. Mentees are those interested in learning from mentors irrespective of their professional status.


How does the mentorship cycle?
Mentees can request for a maximum of 2 mentors from anywhere across the globe, at a go, within a cycle of 1 year. Mentors have specific details of their mode and terms of engagement on their dashboard.


How does the mentorship review system works?
The review system allows mentee to rate mentors on a scale of 100 on their mentorship skills. This rating can guide the actions of prospective mentees, and also assist mentors in improving their skills.


How does the system rewards mentors?
Over time, ratings would be used to nominate mentors for awards and mentorship grants.
